About learning App Development


Believing is seeing
Giving Back Project. I began learning software development few years ago. The journey that featured the darkest period Of time in my life. If I can say it all then you would do shed tears amounting to liters, the pain. Today, When I look back I smile with joy. I tell God thanks for facing it all. If things didn’t go that way then I wouldn’t achieve this great goal. my goal today is to offer help to whoever is learning web development. I do understand perfectly that in order for someone to shine in software engineering one needs a good mentor. personally I have been offered incredible assistance to people whom I have never met physically, living thousands of miles away from me. No matter how I can try I will never be able to payback fully what others have given to me. I am being good to all the way others have been good to me. what is unique is that I have solved a bug that have existed on the internet for decades now. If (at this minute) you make a choice and navigate to your favorite search engine like google and search the following (How to become a software Developer?), you’ll get shocked by the answers being given. 80% of the answers you get is that you start with learning HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, scripting programming languages Like PHP, PYTHON…and query languages of database technology. I agree, this was correct method then when web developers were scarce, one wouldn’t even struggle to get a job. Unlike today when competition is intense, workforce is huge meaning the traditional way of learning web design has transitioned for better. It was so informal that you start learning about an academic discipline without literally defining a tool you’re working with. Any computer in the world understands 0’s and 1’s.If that topic is omitted, how will you be able to fathom the entire discipline of software development. Working without understanding properly what is going on? How will you know about the viruses and their nature? the day you go to the interview panel and be prompted to say something about the digital viruses you seem timid then you realize something is not adding up. Anyway, which panel on earth can trust a developer? who knows nothing about crucial tech knowledge to go on and operate their business websites and applications. The list goes on and on the message is very clearly, you learn from a quack you proceed to be a quack, you learn from the best, you become the best. They said little knowledge is dangerous, they couldn’t have said it any better. when you plan to do something in life, first be willing to pay the huge price to the best of the best. Prepare to be slow and spend the time needed in learning process since that is the time when it matters most. A well-known icon said the following words of wisdom: WHEN YOU ASSIGN HIM WITH THE TASK OF CUTTING A TREE, HE WOULD DO SPEND MOST OF HIS TIME SHARPENING THE AXE. What a time to be alive? All the best in whatever you’re trying to do without life. Never give up no matter how dark the place you’re is expect God to come through for you when it matters most. Eureka happens when you keep pushing. Thanks.
